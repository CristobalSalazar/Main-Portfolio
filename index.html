About

Cristobal Salazar is an accomplished Software Developer and Audio Engineer. Over the course of his career, he has published apps to both the iOS App Store and Google Play Store, built state of the art websites, and showcased knowledge of complex algorithms. While studying Audio production at Humber College, Cristobal started taking interest in the Unity Engine. Curious to see what kind of software he could develop, he quickly realized that he had the passion, discipline and drive to create his own applications. After graduating from Humber, Cristobal enrolled in a certificate course at the University of Toronto for Web Development. Being already comfortable with C#, and having several years of coding experience, it didn’t take long before he added web technologies such as React, Javascript, Node.js, and many others under his belt.

Having a natural attention to detail, everything from design, to logic and audio has been created from scratch and chosen carefully and specifically for each project at hand. If you’re in need of a website to promote your business, showcase your portfolio, bring your app ideas to life, or if you need a self-driven and motivated developer on your team for any web or game development purposes, don’t be afraid to reach out.

Make sure to visit the portfolio section to view examples of work created over the years.
