ABOUT

My name is Cristobal Salazar. I am a self-taught developer and an accomplished music composer, but first and foremost I am a gamer at heart. Ever since I got my hands on an N64 controller at the age of 7, I have always been captivated by the beautiful worlds and feelings that video games can emit. It is my dream and passion to make beautiful art through games.

I began coding at the start of my music degree and haven't looked back since. I love seeing my ideas come to life. Over the course of five years I have become an expert with Unity3D and C# and also picked up some C++ along the way. I am a self-motivated, results oriented person and am never afraid or reluctant to learn new things. I love delving deep into whatever subject is at hand. My areas of expertise are gameplay and sound. But I also love creating VFX with Shaderlab. I have made all the art sounds music and sfx as well as animations for my projects.

I am well versed in a lot of diverse areas within Unity. I have self-published a SHMUP for iOS called Bitstream, and am currently working on a voxel-style ARPG entitled Barb's Adventure. You can check them both out under the "Portfolio" section. They are both also included in my github repository linked below.
